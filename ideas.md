# Gayle Dumbrell Counselling Website - Design Brainstorm

## Design Context
A professional counselling website for children and families. The site must feel **warm, calm, positive, and hopeful** with **accessible, simple, and approachable** language. The audience includes parents seeking support for their children and professionals.

---

## Response 1: Warm Minimalism with Earthy Tones
**Design Movement:** Scandinavian Wellness + Humanist Design  
**Probability:** 0.08

**Core Principles:**
- Generous whitespace with soft, breathing layouts
- Human-centered typography that feels conversational
- Earthy, warm color palette (warm grays, soft terracottas, sage greens)
- Emphasis on clarity and emotional safety through visual simplicity

**Color Philosophy:**
- Primary: Warm sage green (#A8B5A0) - calming, natural, growth-oriented
- Secondary: Soft terracotta (#D4A574) - warmth, approachability, nurturing
- Accent: Cream (#F5F1E8) - softness, safety
- Text: Deep charcoal (#2C2C2C) - readable, grounded
- Rationale: Earthy tones evoke natural environments and emotional stability without feeling clinical

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Asymmetric grid with generous left/right margins
- Hero sections with large typography and breathing room
- Staggered image placement (alternating left/right)
- Soft card-based sections with minimal borders, subtle shadows

**Signature Elements:**
- Hand-drawn line illustrations (simple, organic shapes)
- Soft gradient overlays on images (warm-to-transparent)
- Rounded corners (12-16px) for approachability
- Subtle leaf or growth motifs in decorative elements

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Smooth, gentle transitions (300-400ms easing)
- Hover states with subtle color shifts, not aggressive changes
- Buttons with soft shadows that lift on hover
- Micro-interactions that feel natural and unhurried

**Animation:**
- Fade-in animations on scroll (opacity + slight upward movement)
- Gentle hover effects on cards (shadow increase, slight scale 1.02)
- Smooth scroll behavior throughout
- Subtle parallax on hero images (slow, minimal movement)

**Typography System:**
- Display: Playfair Display (serif, elegant, warm) for headings
- Body: Lato (sans-serif, friendly, readable) for content
- Hierarchy: H1 (48px), H2 (36px), H3 (24px), Body (16px)
- Line height: 1.6-1.8 for readability and breathing room

## Response 2: Modern Wellness with Soft Gradients
**Design Movement:** Contemporary Wellness + Soft Modernism  
**Probability:** 0.07

**Core Principles:**
- Soft, layered gradients creating depth without harshness
- Modern sans-serif typography with intentional hierarchy
- Pastel color palette (soft blues, lavenders, peachy tones)
- Emphasis on emotional connection through visual softness

**Color Philosophy:**
- Primary: Soft sky blue (#7BA8D1) - calm, trust, safety
- Secondary: Soft lavender (#C8B6DB) - gentle, supportive, introspective
- Accent: Warm peach (#F4D4C8) - warmth, approachability
- Background gradient: Cream to soft blue (#F9F7F4 → #EEF3F8)
- Rationale: Soft gradients and pastels create a soothing, modern aesthetic without feeling clinical

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Centered hero with gradient background
- Flowing sections with organic wave dividers
- Image galleries with soft overlays and gradient frames
- Floating card layouts with subtle depth

**Signature Elements:**
- Soft gradient overlays on all images
- Wave/curved SVG dividers between sections
- Rounded buttons with gradient backgrounds
- Subtle animated background elements (floating shapes)

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Smooth, flowing interactions with ease-out timing
- Buttons with gradient shifts on hover
- Cards with shadow elevation on hover
- Floating animations on background elements

**Animation:**
- Fade and slide animations on scroll
- Gradient color shifts on hover
- Floating animations on decorative elements (2-3s duration)
- Smooth page transitions with fade effects

**Typography System:**
- Display: Poppins (bold, modern, friendly) for headings
- Body: Open Sans (clean, accessible, warm) for content
- Hierarchy: H1 (52px), H2 (40px), H3 (28px), Body (16px)
- Letter spacing: Slightly increased for modern feel

## Selected Design Approach: **Warm Minimalism with Earthy Tones**

I've chosen **Response 1: Warm Minimalism with Earthy Tones** for this website.

**Why this approach:**
- The earthy color palette (sage green, soft terracotta, cream) naturally evokes calm, safety, and growth—perfect for a counselling service
- Scandinavian minimalism ensures clarity and accessibility, making the site easy to navigate for all users
- Generous whitespace and asymmetric layouts create breathing room, reflecting the "calm, supportive space" that Gayle offers
- The humanist typography (Playfair + Lato) feels warm and approachable, not clinical
- Hand-drawn elements add personality while maintaining professionalism
- Gentle animations and interactions feel natural and unhurried, aligning with the counselling philosophy

**Design Philosophy for Implementation:**
Every design decision will reinforce the core message: *"This is a safe, warm, and supportive space for your family."* The visual language will be consistent across all pages, using the earthy palette, generous spacing, and warm typography to build trust and emotional connection.
